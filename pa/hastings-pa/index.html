Hastings, PA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Hastings is located in northwestern Cambria County at 40°39′56″N 78°42′43″W (40.665473, -78.711903). It is in the valley of Brubaker Run, a tributary of Chest Creek, which in turn flows northward to the West Branch Susquehanna River.
