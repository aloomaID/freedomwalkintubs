Planada, CA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Planada (Spanish for 'Plain') is a census-designated place (CDP) in Merced County, California, United States. Planada is 9 miles (14 km) east of Merced, the county seat, at an elevation of 226 feet (69 m). The population was 4,164 at the 2020 census, down from 4,584 at the 2010 census.
